Smart farming

Smart farming is a practice that uses technology such as IoT, drones, robotics, machinery, and artificial intelligence to manage farms and increase productivity while reducing environmental impacts. It aims to optimize human labor and ensure the best quality and quantity of farming products. Smart farming is becoming more common among farmers and high-tech farms are quickly becoming the standard
